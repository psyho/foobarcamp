Open Spaces

Open Spaces (un)conference on topics related to software engineering, coding, naming, process management, you name it.

If you have never been to an unconference, the idea is very simple. It comes from an observation, that the most interesting things on conferences happen when people talk during the coffee breaks, so why not organize a conference that is more about coffee breaks than the talks.
