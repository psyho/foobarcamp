Open space?

If you have never been to an open space event, the idea is very simple. It comes from an observation that the most interesting things during conferences happen when people talk during coffee breaks -- so why not organize a conference that is more about coffee breaks than the talks?

For more information about the format watch this excellent video by Bruce Eckel.

If you already have a topic in mind, write a few sentences about it and send them to us -- we will publish it here and you will get a discount!
